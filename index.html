SALINEIQ

AN IOT BASED SALINE MONITORING SYSTEM

INTUITIVE

To provide an saline sensor device for the clinics and general hospitals
in a cost ​efficient way ,and to help doctors and nurses to get the real time information on the saline readings using there devices.

Components/Softwares Used:-

-Ardiuno uno
-Esp32 38bit
-ultra sonics sensors

-IR sensors

Advantages and features of SalineIQ:-

Real-time Monitoring:Continuous tracking of saline concentration levels in medical solutions or patient body fluids. Integration with displays or apps to show live readings.
 Alerting System: Alerts for high or low saline concentrations, notifying healthcare providers when levels fall outside of safe ranges. Configurable thresholds for different medical conditions. 
Calibration and Maintenance Support:
Built-in support for sensor calibration to ensure accurate readings.
Automated or manual calibration options.
Maintenance reminders and diagnostics to ensure sensor accuracy and longevity.
data logging: reads the real time data and updates it to the doctors or nurses on there mobile devices.
